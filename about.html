Memory Palace is the ongoing project of artist Fraser Scowen. Taking inspiration from various fields of thought, art and design.

The project is split into visual works constructed in many different mediums, which present abstractions of memories and of time.

The other part encompassing audio experiments and recorded sound works, with a focus on process, patterns in repetition and experience of sound relative to time.

Other ongoing works include development of a theory for a philosophic approach to modular synthesis and creating collections of visual art works and prints.
